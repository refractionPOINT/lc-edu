The Add-ons Marketplace enables LimaCharlie users to subscribe to various APIs, Lookups and Services at the click of a button.

APIs are deep integrations and enable core capabilities, along with other superpowers that are close to the metal.

Services are lighter integrations and cover a wide range of uses. They can also be created as public or private by any LimaCharlie user (for fun or profit).

Lookups are simply that: threat feeds, IP reputation, known bad hash lists - basically anything that can be represented as a dictionary. Can be used directly in detection and response rules logic.

Add-ons are applied on a per tenant basis.

LimaCharlie's Infrastructure as Code (IaC) approach can be used to create tenants with pre-configured Add-on configurations.
