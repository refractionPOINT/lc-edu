Welcome to Advanced Detection & Response (DR).

This course is designed to build the strong foundation established in the course Basic Detection & Response.

At its most basic level DR is used to secure computational assets by monitoring Events for indicators of compromise (IoC). When an IoC is detected a response action is triggered. Response actions range from firing an alert to isolating the given host from the network.

This course examines several advanced mechanisms available to the rule author in defending against advanced persistent threats and automating security operations.
