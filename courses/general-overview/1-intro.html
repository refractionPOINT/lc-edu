This is an introductory survey course for the LimaCharlie Security Infrastructure as a Service (SIaaS) platform. This course is designed to give the student a high-level overview of the architecture and capabilities of LimaCharlie.

At the end of this course the user will:

  • Have a clear understanding of what LimaCharlie is and the scope of its capabilities.
  • Understand the basica principles and mechanisms that make up the LimaCharlie SIaaS platform.

Any questions relating to this course, or LimaCharlie in general, can be directed towards answers@limacharlie.io.
