This is an introductory course for the LimaCharlie Security Infrastructure as a Service (SIaaS) platform. This course is designed to give the student a high-level overview of the architecture and capabilities of LimaCharlie.

At the end of this course the user will:

  • Have a clear understanding of what LimaCharlie is and the scope of its capabilities.
  • Understand the basic principles and mechanisms that make up the LimaCharlie SIaaS platform.
