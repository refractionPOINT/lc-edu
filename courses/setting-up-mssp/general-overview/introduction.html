This course outlines best practices for the most efficient use of LimaCharlie at scale. This structure is ideal for an MSSP or SOC that is managing multiple organizations but offers many benefits to all classes of LimaCharlie users.

LimaCharlie provides full control over its technology. With this control LimaCharlie users are able to adapt to a wide variety of environments and technologies. This permissive approach is extremely powerful and also why the way that deployments are structured is important so that users can avoid problems as they scale.

The architectural approach outlined in this course not only will help avoid issues at scale but will also provide a scaffold which makes it easy to onboard new customer organizations.

A public repository with the assets used in this course can be found here: https://github.com/refractionPOINT/mssp-demo
